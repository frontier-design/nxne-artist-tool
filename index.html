How to Get Started

1. Upload your image and audio using the menu in the top left

2. Replace Insert Your Artist Name with your name

3. Choose a colour that fits well with your image

4. You can generate different layouts using the Refresh button

5. Once you have a layout you like, you can download the image or in both Post and Reel dimensions

6. Post the image to your socials and let people know you're playing NXNE!

Suggested hashtags: #NXNE2025, #MUSICFEST, #SOUNDON
