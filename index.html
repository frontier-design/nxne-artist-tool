How to Get Started

1. Upload your image using the 'Upload Image' button in the top left

2. Replace 'Insert Artist Name Here' with your name

3. You can regenerate different layouts using the 'Refresh' button

4. Once you have a layout you like, you can Download the image in both IG Post and IG Story dimensions

5. Post the image to your socials and let people know you're playing NXNE!

Suggested hashtags: #NXNE2025, #MUSICFEST, #SOUNDON
